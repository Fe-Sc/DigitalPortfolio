So, who am i?

My name is Fernando Schintz, and i study Creative Technology at the University of Twente.

I am a Creative Technology student exploring the intersection of design, technology and art. I am interested in how interactive systems can shape experiences and influence the way people engage with the world around them.

Throughout my studies, i have been developing skills in programming, prototyping and digital design, while learning to translate ideas into tangible concepts.

Beyond academics, i have a strong interest in music and media, which influences the way i think creatively and approach design challenges.

I am actively building my professional network and open to collaborations, new ideas and opportunities that allow me to grow as a designer and technologist.

Feel free to connect with me, or read more about me and my projects in my portfolio!
