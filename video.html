Video for behaviour change

A project where i learnt to use Davinchi Resolve to edit a video promoting and encouraging positve behaviour change.

I also learnt to do proper storyboarding, brainstorming and basic videography skills.

This project was made for the Design for Behaviour Change part of Introduction to CreaTe in the first module of the first year of my Creative Technology study.

We were tasked to choose a behaviour we wanted to promote and encourage, and create a short video that would effectively communicate our message.

We chose to discourage the behaviour of gambling and how devastating the consequences of it can be.
